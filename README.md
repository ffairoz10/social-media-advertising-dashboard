# social-media-advertising-dashboard
Paid Social Performance Quaterly Dashboard 
A Power BI-built editorial-style quarterly analytics brief that examines the performance of paid social media advertising across six platforms, 280 campaigns, and $2.33 billion in spending.

**Key Insights**
*$2.33B total spend across 255,092 campaigns in 2022
*Instagram leads ROAS at 4.01X - closely followed by Twitter (4.00X) and Facebook (3.99X)
*Pinterest underperforms — 25% spend share but only 0.72X ROAS, driven by low CVR despite high impressions
*CTR is nearly identical across Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter (~32.93%) — indicating consistent audience targeting efficiency
*Los Angeles is the top city by conversions at 88.28M, followed by Miami (87.45M) and New York (87M)
*Brand Awareness campaigns lead all goals at 109.61M conversions, marginally ahead of Product Launch (108.93M)
*Women aged 45–60 show the lowest conversion volume (47.87M) while Men aged 45–60 show the highest (48.97M)
